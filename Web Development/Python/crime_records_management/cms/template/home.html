Crime Records Management System

The Crime Records Management System is a secure digital platform designed to streamline law enforcement data, including crime reports, investigations, and charge sheets. It enhances efficiency, ensuring accurate and quick access to records while promoting transparency and security in the justice system.

Key Features

  • Centralized database for crime records
  • Role-based access control
  • Real-time tracking of investigations
  • Secure and encrypted data storage
  • Efficient case management & charge sheet generation
